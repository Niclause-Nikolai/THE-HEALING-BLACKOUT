What is?
The Healing Blackout
A Global movement where it asks the people to turn off the lights for an hour that represents a call to action regarding climate change and the environment. This is not only a symbolic gesture but rather calls one to real action with environmental issues we experience in current times, including pollution and wasteful consumption of energy and usage of natural resources to mention but a few. Further encouraged through this campaign are discussions surrounding sustainable practices and solutions to the climate to inspire long-term change in habits and policies toward it.

Goal To encourage all the youths and also adults to reduce energy consumption by turning off lights during Earth Hour. This initiative want to create sustainable habits and long-term benefits for the environment that can be beneficial for the next generation
PURPOSE
IMPACTS
OF THE HEALING BLACKOUT
The act of turning off lights during Earth Hour has reduced energy consumption, decreased carbon emissions, improved air quality, and helped protect natural resources, such as water and fossil fuels. Moreover, through this global movement, millions of people from different cultures and backgrounds with a sense of responsibility and a hope for a better future are united.

WHY DO WE NEED
THIS ADVOCACY
1. Limit Pollution By saving energy we thus reduce harmful emissions from fossil fuel-powered electrical plants, thus providing more transparent air and a cleaner surrounding

2. Heal the Ozone Layer Every little move makes a difference in reducing depletion caused to the ozone layer-which shields us against negative ultraviolet rays

3. Sustainable Lighting During the Earth Hour, LED Lights are a perfect replacement where energy consumption is minimal; while it lasts longer than bulbs or incandescent lights.

4. Conserve Energy Switch off unnecessary lights to lower the electricity bill and save limited resources, which will leave an impact on the planet as well as your pocket for good.
